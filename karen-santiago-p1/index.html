How to install Mods

Step One: Go into the game and enable 'Custom Content' and 'Mods & Script Mods Allowed' in game settings.
Step Two: Close the game.
Step Three: Grab the downloaded mod, then open Electronic Art & then Sims 4 folder
Step Four: Open the Mod folder within the Sims 4 folder.
Step Five: Drop downloaded mod into the mod folder.
